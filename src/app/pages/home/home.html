Across Volusia County, people of every background, belief and walk of life are coming together in homes, parks and community spaces to learn, to worship, and to serve — and to discover what we can build when we see one another as members of a single family.

The Bahá’í Faith

A faith built on oneness.

The Bahá’í Faith is among the youngest of the world’s independent religions. Founded by Bahá’u’lláh (1817–1892), its central theme is the oneness of humanity — that the earth is but one country, and its peoples one family, called to build a peaceful and ever-advancing global civilization.

Throughout history, God has sent a succession of divine Educators — the Manifestations of God — whose teachings are successive chapters of one unfolding religion.
